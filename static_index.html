Popular Diet Types

Understand different nutrition approaches to reach your goals

Keto Diet

High-fat, low-carb diet that aims to induce ketosis for fat burning.

Food Sources:
  • Meat, fish, eggs
  • Butter, cheese, nuts
  • Avocados, seeds
  • Low-carb vegetables
High-Carb Diet

Higher proportion of carbohydrates for energy and performance.

Food Sources:
  • Whole grains (oats, quinoa)
  • Fruits and vegetables
  • Legumes (beans, lentils)
  • Potatoes, rice
Low-Carb Diet

Restricts carbohydrate intake for weight loss and blood sugar control.

Food Sources:
  • Non-starchy vegetables
  • Meat, fish, eggs
  • Nuts and seeds
  • Berries (in moderation)
Intermittent Fasting

Cycling between periods of eating and fasting (e.g., 16:8 method).

During Eating Windows:
  • Lean protein sources
  • Healthy fats
  • Fruits and vegetables
  • Balanced meals
