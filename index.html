Diet Information

Explore different diet approaches to achieve your goals

Ketogenic Diet

A high-fat, low-carb diet that induces ketosis, helping your body burn fat for fuel instead of carbohydrates.

Food Sources:
Meat Fish Eggs Cheese Nuts Avocados

High-Carb Diet

A diet with higher carbohydrate proportions, ideal for athletes and those with high energy demands.

Food Sources:
Oats Quinoa Brown Rice Fruits Legumes Potatoes

Low-Carb Diet

Restricts carbohydrate intake for weight loss and blood sugar control while maintaining protein and fat intake.

Food Sources:
Leafy Greens Broccoli Meat Fish Nuts Berries

Intermittent Fasting

Cycling between periods of eating and fasting, with popular methods like 16:8 or 18:6 fasting windows.

During Eating Windows:
Lean Protein Healthy Fats Vegetables Fruits Whole Grains
